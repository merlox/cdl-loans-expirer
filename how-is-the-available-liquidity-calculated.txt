If there's no amount supplied, AvailableLiquidity is 0

If 1 user added liquidity, the AvailableLiquidity is the added liquidity

If the user added liquidity AND loaned the funds for a loan to a user, the AvailableLiquidity will be funds NOT loaned, meaning AvailableLiquidity will be totalFundsAdded - fundsLoaned

The crypto score is never taken into consideration
